About us

MO CAPITAL is a registered Black Female owned Micro lending business. MO CAPITAL was founded in 2018 and established in 2021. MO CAPITAL is registered with the National Credit Regulator and in tune with all the regulations stated by the NCR, this allows us to lend money in a more smooth and easy way at low interest rates.
The aim is to create quick and easy access to short term loans at low interest rates for those in need. Our Micro lending process is made easier in such a way that our clients can process the loans applications with their mobile phone.
We pride ourselves in becoming one of the biggest financial service provider in the country

How it works

Step 1

Send through required documents (3 Month bank statement, most recent Pay slip and ID copy)

Step 2

Your documents will be checked for loan approval.

Step 3

Once approved, you will receive an SMS with details to approve Debicheck

Step 4

Log into your banking app and approve Debicheck, or alternatively use the USSD option. Full details will be included in the SMS.

Step 5

Payment will be sent into your bank account.

Step 6

It's important to approve the Debicheck, or else payment will not be successful.
